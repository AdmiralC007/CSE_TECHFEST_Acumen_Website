Free Fire

Tournament Format:
• Mode: Squad
• Maps: Bermuda, Purgatory
• Elimination: First round -> Final round

First Round:
We divide the registered teams into groups (Like Group A and Group B) based on the number of teams registered. The groups will play one Bermuda match. From each group, top teams will qualify for the next round (No points calculation, just top teams from each will qualify).

Final Round:
The qualified teams will play two full maps (Bermuda and Purgatory). Top two teams will be decided based on points calculation.

Points Calculation:
Per kill - 1 point
Booyah - 12 points
#2 - 9 points
#3 - 8 points
#4 - 7 points
#5 - 6 points
#6 - 5 points
#7 - 4 points
#8 - 3 points
#9 - 2 points
#10 - 1 point
#11 and #12 - 0 points

For example, if 20 teams are registered, we divide those teams into two groups (10 teams per group). The two groups will play a Bermuda match, then the top 6 teams in each group (based on position) will qualify for the final round. Then the competition continues as described in the Final Round.
