THREE LEGGED MAZE RACE

🦵 Round 1: The Three-Legged Race
👫 Each team pairs up and ties one of their legs together.
🏃‍♂ Players must race through a marked course while staying in perfect rhythm.
🔥 Round 2: The Lava Path Challenge
⚡ Navigate across a "lava field" using only designated stepping stones.
🥓 Round 3: Steal the Bacon
🎯 A numbered object ("bacon") is placed at the center of the field.
🔢 When a number is called, the corresponding player must sprint, grab the bacon, and return to their side before the opponent does!
