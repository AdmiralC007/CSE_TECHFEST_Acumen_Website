BID 'N' CODE

Are you ready for an electrifying battle where strategy meets coding? Bid & Code is not just about solving problems—it’s about making bold moves, outbidding your competitors, and proving your coding prowess! Whether you’re a first-year beginner or a seasoned third-year coder, this event is designed to give everyone a fair and thrilling experience.
Why Should You Participate?
Separate Prizes for Each Year! No need to worry about competing against seniors or juniors—first years, second years, and third years will have separate leaderboards and prizes!
Exclusive First-Year Questions! To ensure an equal playing field, first-year students will get a special set of questions tailored to their level.
Exciting Strategy-Based Bidding System! It's not just about coding—it’s about choosing your battles wisely.
Event Structure
Round 1: The Qualifier Quiz
A fun and challenging quiz to test fundamental coding knowledge.
Top teams from each year will qualify for the bidding round.
Round 2: The Bidding War
Teams will be given fixed bid points at the start.
Questions of varying difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard) will be displayed.
Teams must strategically bid their points to win the right to solve a question.
First-year students will have separate questions to ensure a balanced challenge.
Round 3: The Coding Showdown
Teams who win their bids will solve the problem within a set time.
The fastest and most accurate solutions will determine the winners.
