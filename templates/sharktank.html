SHARK TANK CHALLENGE

🚀 Think you’ve got the brains to outsmart, outbid, and outpitch the competition? Welcome to the Shark Tank Challenge, a one-of-a-kind event where strategy, creativity, and business acumen collide!
From a high-stakes quiz to a wild bidding war and an epic Shark Tank-style pitch, this event is designed to test your wits, strategy, and persuasion skills.
💰 Registration Fee: ₹100 per team
🏆 Prize Pool: ₹1000
👥 Team Size: 2-3 members
⚡ The 3-Round Showdown
📚 Round 1: The Knowledge Bid – Win Your Credits!
A fast-paced quiz where quick thinking earns you bidding credits.
The better you perform, the more credits you’ll have for the next round!
💸 Round 2: The Reverse Auction – The Ultimate Mind Game!
Get ready for the most thrilling and unpredictable bidding war! 🧠💰
🔹 Lowest unique bid wins – So don’t go too high or too low!
🔹 Tied bids get canceled – If two teams bid the same amount, both lose!
🔹 Surprise Multipliers 🎲 – Some teams may randomly receive a mystery credit boost or penalty to shake things up!
🔹 Stealth Mode 🕶 – Each team gets one chance to place a hidden bid, keeping their move secret until the results are revealed!
🔹 The Shark’s Gamble 🦈 – The team with the most remaining credits after bidding can steal and modify a problem statement before Round 3!
Will you bluff, risk, or play it safe? It’s all about strategy!
🎤 Round 3: The Pitch Arena – Impress the Sharks!
Time to pitch your winning idea in Shark Tank style! 🦈
You’ll get 5 minutes to showcase your solution, innovation, and marketability.
The judges (Sharks) will grill you – so be ready to defend your idea!
The best pitch wins the grand prize!
