About Our HOD

Prof. T. Adilakshmi

Dr. T. Adilakshmi has over 34 years of teaching and research experience, currently serving as the HoD of the CSE Department at Vasavi College of Engineering. She holds a B.Tech in ECE from VCE, an M.Tech in CSE from MIT Mangalore, and a Ph.D. in AI from Hyderabad Central University. A distinguished academician, she has guided multiple Ph.D. scholars and published 154 research papers in leading journals and conferences. Recognized with prestigious awards, including the Best HoD of the Year (2018) and Best Teacher Award (2005), she has played a pivotal role in enhancing research, placements, and industry collaboration, establishing the Industrial IoT Lab under AICTE MODROB.
