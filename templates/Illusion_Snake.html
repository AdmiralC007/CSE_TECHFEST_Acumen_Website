HUMAN SIZED SNAKE & LADDER

Get ready for a life-sized twist on the classic game! HUMAN-SIZED SNAKES & LADDERS is a fun and thrilling event where you become the player on a giant board. Climb the ladders, dodge the snakes, and race your way to victory!
🎯 Event Format:
Roll the dice 🎲, move on the giant board, climb the ladders, and beware of the snakes! 🐍
🌟Special challenge squares may include fun tasks or dares to proceed.
The team that reaches the finish line in the given time limit first wins! 🏆
📌 Event Details:
Team Size: 2 people per team
Registration Fee: 100/- per team
