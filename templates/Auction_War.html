AUCTION WAR

A high-energy bidding game where teams compete to win mystery boxes containing hidden point values.
Each team starts with a limited bidding budget and must strategize to outbid rivals while managing their resources wisely.
The auctioneer conducts live bidding, creating an atmosphere of excitement and suspense.
Once a team wins a box, they reveal its contents, which could be high points, low points, or unexpected twists.
The game continues for multiple rounds, and at the end, the team with the highest total score from their boxes wins.
The thrill lies in the unpredictability—every bid is a risk, and every box holds a surprise.
